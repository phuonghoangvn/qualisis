Interviewer:

Thank you for speaking with me today. Could you tell me about a time recently when you felt particularly overwhelmed?

Participant (P1):

Well, last Tuesday was a nightmare. I had three students absent who needed makeup packets, my principal asked for the quarter grades early, and then during my prep period, a parent called to complain about a B minus.

I just felt this deep sense of exhaustion where my chest gets tight. It's like no matter how much I prep, the expectations just keep shifting.

Interviewer:

How did you handle that feeling in the moment?

Participant (P1):

Honestly, I went to my car during lunch and just sat in silence for 15 minutes. It's the only place where no one needs anything from me. You learn these little micro-coping strategies to survive till Friday.
